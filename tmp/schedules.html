Cricket is among the popular sports in Pakistan and different parts of the world. It is a reason that International Cricket Council or ICC arranges contests between its member countries. ICC Cricket World Cup is their biggest event that is held after every four years. However, ICC Cricket World Cup 2025 is scheduled in India. On the other side, ICC T20 World Cup is held after every two years. As per the ICC cricket schedule, it will take place in India in October and November. The T20 World Cup 2025 schedule is not confirmed yet. However, you will be able to see all key details once ICC will announce the fixtures.

Pakistan Super League is the most followed cricket event of Pakistan. Pakistan Cricket Board or PCB organizes it every year. Karachi Kings, Lahore Qalandars, Islamabad United, Peshawar Zalmi, Quetta Gladiators, and Multan Sultans participate in the event.
